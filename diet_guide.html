DIET GUIDELINES:
Paddy Rice, Wheat,and vegetables can be taken as much as required.
Salt, Sour, Tamarind, Chilies having less pungency can be taken in mild quantity just to add a taste of the food and not to the fullest satisfaction of the tongue.
After the intake of Amrita Sanjeevani (Amaraalaya), the herbal medicine, following food recommendations should be followed strictly for the best results.
Day-1 (On the day you have taken the herbal medicine)
For Breakfast -After one hour you can have porridge(kanji) of Rice and Green dhal cooked together without adding salt. ( Kanji is a preparation of rice cooked in water and the water is not drained away after the rice is cooked. This Rice cooked water is a healthy drink too.) You may add little-scrubbed coconut for taste.
For Lunch- the Same porridge can be taken for the noon, but little salt can be added to it.
For Supper- Normal porridge of Rice with little green dhal cooked as the side dish ( with moderate salt)
Day-2
After taking a bath, the patient may take preparation made out of Rice powder (Puttu, Idiyappam,etc..).Dhosha made out of wheat powder can also be taken.
Items containing Urad Dhal such as Idli,Dosha should be avoided.
Second day noon, along with Rice, side dishes made out of green leafy vegetables and other vegetables like raw banana, Ladies finger, beans items etc.. can be taken.
Second day Night also same things can be taken. Strictly avoid sour items and tamarind.
Day-3
The third day until the 7th day, add salt, tamarind/ sour things, chilies in very moderate level (little) with vegetable side dishes .Absolutely No Turmeric and Oil .
Consume a lot of Cumin seed water throughout the day. Daily a minimum of 4-5 liters of boiled and cool water should be consumed.
Vegetables that can be consumed.
Drumsticks, Small onions, leafy vegetables, cucumber, beans items,carrot, long Bananas, Banana trunk, Banana flower, etc…
Vegetables that are to be avoided:
Brinjal, Agasthi leaves,Drumstick leaves, tomato, pumpkins, bitter guard, Snake guard, big onions, cabbage, beetroot, potato, Sambar dhal etc…
IN GENERAL , FOLLOWING .ITEMS ARE TO BE AVOIDED FOR THE BEST RESULT.
1.Coffee, Tea,Milk
2.Eatables that contain Urad dhal
3.Oily food
4.Fried items
5.All Root items ( Except Carrot)
6.No Smoking, Pan, Intoxicants, tobacco items
7.Pan Parag and other related chewable,
8.Allopathic medicines/Antibiotic items
9.Shaving also should be avoided during this period.
Following items are to be avoided for a minimum of Three years.
Dry fish, Jack fruit, Broiler chicken, beef etc..
On the day of the intake of the Amritha Sanjeevini (Amaraalaya) herbal medicine, the patient may feel overly tired and can take a maximum of one hour nap during the day time.
But from the next day onwards no sleeping during the day time. However one should sleep well during the nights. From the fourth day of the consumption of the herbal medicine, fruits like Apple, Musambi can be taken.
